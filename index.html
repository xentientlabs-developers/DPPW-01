About Chrissi

After spending more than 10 years as a tv anchor and reporter, Chrissi decided she wanted to spend more time with her young children.   Now that they’re in school, Chrissi has used her talents to become an effective spokesperson for a number of area businesses.   Chrissi has pitched everything from cars to furniture to grills.   Her ease in front of the camera and a microphone makes her a strong salesperson for any product.
